Our Services

Our dedicated team of experts are ready and able to help you from the beginning of your project conception till, planning stages, project management, deployment, and making sure your site is visible and attracts the clients you want!

Project Management

Manage all aspects of your web s from beginning to end. Web projects are ALL about the small details! We make sure those details do not get lost, ignored, or overlooked.

Design

Bring all your ideas to life with colorful, user-friendly, UI/UX designs that will help outline your project visually so that the changes you want can be done before we even begin to develop.

Development

Experienced front-end and back-end developers that can make any idea a reality. No project is too big or too small for this team of expert developers at Devonshire Dev Shop!

Launch

After all of the details of your web projects have been finalized and green-lighted, our expert dev-ops engineers will upload your baby into your preferred hosting cloud and out into the world.

Analytics

In order to make sure your web project is hitting all the right benchmarks, our analytics team will you set up, install, and understand your preferred analytics tool.

SEO

What is your web project worth if no one can find it easily online or if it doesn't get the traffic it deserves? Our SEO experts can conduct a full-site audit to make sure Google and other web browsers are indexing your site correctly and rank you higher.

About Us

Devonshire Dev Shop was founded in 2024 out of a necessity to provide the people and organizations close to us with the great, quality dev work at a fraction of the going rate and time. We wanted to create a team that people can trust, a process that organizations can sign off on, and a human feeling to what is quickly becoming a place devoid of humanity; the web.

At Devonshire, we believe in:

  • Honesty
  • Integrity
  • Tranparency
  • Client Empowerment
  • Communication
  • Clarity
  • Efficiency
  • Education

From the moment we meet our potential clients, we strive to create a lasting bond that comes from a deep understanding of their needs, concerns, and dreams. We know that tech/digital/the web can be scary concepts and strive to educate and empower our clients to take as much control of their digital projects and assets so they feel comfortable using them on their owns and maximizing their potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Devonshire Dev Shop is always here for all your questions and concerns. We know development can be stressful and overwhelming, here are some frequently asked questions and answers to help put your mind at ease.

1. How do I know you will do a good job on my site?

At Devonshire, we value the customer’s peace of mind. From the beginning of the design/development process, our detail oriented Project Manager will ask all the necessary questions in order to make sure we understand your vision perfectly. During all the following phases of work such as design, development, and launch you will have FULL TRANSPARENCY of every stage in the form of design files and staging links. We will NOT move forward to the next phase of the process without your full written consent.

2. How long does each digital project take?

This question is highly dependent on what the project is. Some projects such as content edits take very little time and some projects like the building of a site from scratch may take up to a few months. This factor is also dependent on the level of cooperation we receive from our clients, as we need their feedback, information, and consent in order to ensure the project is as they wish it to be. In general, we at Devonshire Dev Shop make a VERY BIG EFFORT to finish our tasks as soon as possible so you can start profiting from them.

3. What is the cost for these digital projects?

This question is highly dependent on what the project is and the time spent on it. At Devonshire, before we begin work on any site we make sure that we work out a price per hour or project that will make both sides happy. We then provide our clients with the estimated time and cost in a project proposal document for them to approve.

4. Why should I trust you with my site?

Great question! The highly reliable and experienced team of developers, project managers, and dev ops engineers at Devonshire Dev Shop have been involved in thousands of websites and other digital projects over the past decade with great success and customer reviews. Furthermore, each project comes with a Satisfaction Guarantee that makes sure you are happy with the result.

5. What is the process of development like?

The first step to building your perfect digital project is booking a FREE, 30-minute discovery call with our talented Project Manager, Eyal. He will listen to you attentively while you describe your project visions, needs, functions, and future aspirations. Before, after, or during the call, you will be prompted to fill out a brief intake survey that will help us understand your project even better.
